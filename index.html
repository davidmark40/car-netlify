What’s the Cost of Renting a Car in Dubai?

One of the most common questions people have is, “How much does it cost to rent a car in Dubai?” The truth is, rental prices can vary widely depending on several factors, including the type of car, rental duration, and the season.

  • Economy Cars: If you’re on a tight budget, you can expect to pay around AED 80 to AED 150 per day for a compact or economy car. These cars are ideal for navigating the city and getting from point A to point B without breaking the bank.
  • Luxury Cars: Dubai is the land of luxury, so why not treat yourself to a stylish ride? Luxury cars like BMW, Mercedes, and Audi typically start at around AED 300 to AED 600 per day, depending on the model and availability.
  • SUVs and Vans: If you need more space or want to explore the desert, expect to pay around AED 200 to AED 400 per day for an SUV or a 7-seater van.

Don’t forget that many rental companies offer discounts for longer rental periods. So, the more days you rent, the better the deal you can get!
